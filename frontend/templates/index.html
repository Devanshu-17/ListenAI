Some of our Main Features

Key Features of Our Tool

Meet ListenAI, the mental health tool that answers the skyrocketing need for mental health care, and breaks down the systemic constraints that block equal access to it. Designed by humans, powered by AI, and grounded in science, ListenAI easily integrates with health systems to provide evidenced-based behavioral health solutions that get people off a waitlist, and onto a path to feeling better.

Intelligent Voice Powered Conversations

By effortlessly translating spoken words into meaningful responses with the help of Whisper, ListenAI offers a natural and intuitive communication experience, making conversations with the chatbot as effortless as speaking to a friend.

Answer patient's questions 24/7

Your round-the-clock healthcare companion, providing answers and care whenever you need them. With our integrated ListenAI Whatsapp Bot, you can get help with your mental health anytime, anywhere.

Multilingual Support

Empower your conversations with our chatbot's seamless Multilingual Support, fueled by advanced LLMs for clear and effective responses across diverse languages.

Life changes. ListenAI does, too.

With mental health needs in constant flux, our distinctive strategy ensures both impactful outcomes and ongoing enhancement. ListenAI stands ready to provide timely and tailored support, making a difference precisely when it matters most.

Open Source Integrated

Embrace our cost-effective solution as we integrate the open-source LLAMA 2 model by Meta, ensuring efficient AI capabilities. Your data remains private, safeguarding your confidentiality and trust.

AI Tailored to You

Tailor your chatbot to embody your organization's essence. Our solution empowers customization of responses, behavior, and language, seamlessly aligning with your branding.

Global Reach, Local Connection

Break language barriers effortlessly with our chatbot's multilingual prowess. Whether it's English, Spanish, or any language in between, our solution empowers your organization to connect with users worldwide in their preferred language.

Couple that with the power to choose between OpenAI GPT and Meta's LLAMA 2 models for responses, ensuring tailor-made conversations that resonate with your audience.

Whisper of Innovation

Voice interactions are the heart of human communication, and we've harnessed that essence with our Whisper model integration. Elevate engagement and accessibility with voice-enabled interactions, crafting conversations that flow naturally.


Imagine users effortlessly seeking assistance with a simple command, where technology becomes an invisible assistant, always ready to provide solutions.

Your Chatbot, Your Brand
